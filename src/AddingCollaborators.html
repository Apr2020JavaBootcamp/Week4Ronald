Adding collaborators to your Github repositories

For group projects, knowing how to take advantage of Github's collaborators feature is essential. A collaborator is just someone you give permission to push/pull from a given repository. Follow these easy steps to invite collaborators for your projects:

  1. Open the repository you wish to add collaborators on
  2. On the second line after the nav bar at the end, select 'Settings'
  3. Once in settings, from the vertical menu on the left select 'Manage access'
  4. Almost at the bottom of the screen there should be a box with a title that reads 'Manage access'. Find a green button that reads 'Invite a Collaborator' inside this box and select it.
  5. Now provide the Github information of your collaborators and send the invite. They will receive an email that will grant them access to your repository.
